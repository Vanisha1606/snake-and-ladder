How to Play

🔒

Roll a 6 to start

Every player begins locked. You must roll a six to enter the board on tile 1.

🎲

Roll the dice

Tap the dice on your turn. Move your token forward by the rolled number.

🪜

Climb ladders

Land on the bottom of a ladder and zoom up to its top.

🐍

Avoid snakes

Land on a snake's head and slide all the way down to its tail.

🎯

Reach 100 exactly

The first player to land exactly on tile 100 wins. Overshooting? Stay put.

⭐

Roll a 6, go again

Rolling a 6 grants you another roll. Three sixes in a row resets the turn.
